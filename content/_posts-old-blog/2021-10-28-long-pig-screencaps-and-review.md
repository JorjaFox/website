<p>Finally we've narrowed the possible suspects down to one, but to get there, boy did Sara play Nora perfectly! Instead of getting angry (like the CSI Sara of yore), she channeled it into <em>appearing</em> emotional while remaining calm and in control. Sara remained true to herself while being a more balanced person than she was a decade ago.</p>

<p>I have to confess I burst out laughing when she came out of the bedroom in just a robe, and was just so "sigh, this is my husband" at his mini-lab. That is the perfect example of their marriage as I would have dreamed it. I loved it so much ...</p>
