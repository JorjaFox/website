There's a lot of stuff to unpack here!

I love Hugo more and more each week. He's the best of the Quirky Lab Rats. I also adore seeing how Max was training CSI Gill (the young woman) in how to connect evidence to theory properly.

Speaking of evidence, Grissom's adherence to evidence (and robot testimony) is likely to get a hard slap of reality. With things like Deep Fakes and (as we've seen in that storage unit) fake evidence, how much can Grissom actually trust and believe in the evidence anymore?

And to that end, he gets a bit of a reminder that maaaaybe his wife and her faith in people has a point.
